PHYS273 & PHYS273H Introductory Physics: Waves

Course info and Important Updates:

Oscillations and AC circuits using complex variables, Fourier series and integrals, waves on a string, sound; electromagnetic waves from Maxwell's equations
in differential form; physical optics

Lecture: Tuesdays and Thursdays 0930 Hours
Discussion: Fridays 1000 Hours

This website will be updated weekly with notes, Homework Solutions and Important Announcements

Course Outline:

Oscillations:

Topics include mechanical oscillators, oscillating circuits, damoped and driven oscillations and systems of two coupled oscilators. Mathematical tools
introduced deal with these include complex exponentials, Fourier series and Fourier transforms
Reading from Morin: 1.1, 1.2, 1.3, 2.1, 2.2, 2.3, 2.4, 3.1, 3.2, 3.3, 3.4, 3.5.

Waves in 1-Dimension:

Topics include transverse and longitudinal waves in one dimension, traveling and standing waves, energy and momentum in waves, reflection
and transmission, impdence and dispersion.
Reading from Morin: 2.4, 4.1, 4.2, 4.3, 4.4, 4.5, 5.1, 6.1, 6.2, 6.3.

Waves in Higher Dimensions:

Topics include waves in two and three dimensions, plane waves, electromagnetic waves, polarization, the doppler effect, shock waves, interference
and diffraction
Reading from Morin: 7.1, 7.2, 8.1, 8.2, 8.3, 8.4, 8.5, 8.6, 8.7, 8.8, 9.1, 9.2, 9.3.
