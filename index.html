PHYS273 Introductory Physics: Waves

Course info

Oscillations and AC circuits using complex variables, Fourier series and integrals, waves on a string, sound; electromagnetic waves from Maxwell's equations
in differential form; physical optics

Lecture: Tuesdays and Thursdays 0930 Hours
Discussion: Fridays 1000 Hours

Pre-requisites and Co-requisites

Introductory Physics: Fields (PHYS272)
Multivariable Calculus: Calc III (MATH241)

Introduction to Linear Algebra (MATH240)
Or
Linear Algebra for Scientists and Engineers (MATH461)
Or
Mathematica Methods for Physics I (PHYS274)
