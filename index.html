PHYS273 & PHYS273H Introductory Physics: Waves

Course info and Important Updates:

Oscillations and AC circuits using complex variables, Fourier series and integrals, waves on a string, sound; electromagnetic waves from Maxwell's equations
in differential form; physical optics

The official title for this course is introductory physics: waves. In fact, this course is somewhat broader than this. Apart from dealing with the physics of waves and
osciallations, it will introduce a lot of mathematical techniques that will prepare you for higher level courses and real life research.

Advice:

  • The only way to learn anything is to do it; just listening to the instructor/TA's or reading the text, is insufficient.
  • Do derivations yourself, do the homework, keep up with the class, ask questions and come to Office Hours.
  • Avoid the temptation to use online printed solutions. And while it is useful for some people to compare their work to others, solve the
    problem first on your own. You learn physics by solving problems, not by copying them.
